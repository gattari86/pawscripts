Strategic Recommendation

Choose 15-Page If You:

  • Prioritize long-term SEO dominance
  • Have dedicated content resources
  • Need maximum market differentiation
  • Target multiple geographic markets
  • Plan aggressive content marketing

Choose 8-Page If You:

  • Need to launch ASAP
  • Have limited initial budget
  • Want to test market response first
  • Prefer iterative improvement
  • Focus on paid advertising initially

Hybrid Approach:

  • Start with 8-page version
  • Gather user data and feedback
  • Expand to 15 pages over 6 months
  • Add state guides as needed
  • Build out persona pages based on data
